# About FishSmart Pro

## 🌊 Precision Fishing Intelligence

**FishSmart Pro** is an advanced, AI-powered intelligence platform designed for the modern angler who demands data-driven insights. By bridging the gap between marine biology, atmospheric science, and cutting-edge artificial intelligence, we transform raw environmental data into actionable fishing strategies.

### 🧬 The Science: The "Bite Score"
At the heart of FishSmart Pro is our proprietary **Scientific Engine**. Unlike traditional apps that rely on simple calendars, FishSmart Pro calculates a real-time **Bite Score** (Bite Probability) using two primary biological drivers:

1.  **Metabolic Efficiency**: We track current water temperatures against species-specific thermal ranges (Optimal vs. Dormant) to determine the fish's baseline activity level.
2.  **Barometric Pressure Trends**: Our engine analyzes atmospheric pressure to apply scientific multipliers—recognizing that low-pressure systems trigger feeding frenzies while post-frontal high pressure requires finesse.

### 🤖 The Intelligence: AI-Driven Strategy
FishSmart Pro integrates with **Google Gemini AI** to provide more than just numbers. Our AI "Angler's Assistant" synthesizes the scientific data, current weather conditions, and species behavior patterns to recommend:
- **Optimal Lure Selection**: Matching size, color, and vibration to water clarity and fish activity.
- **Structural Targeting**: Identifying exactly where fish are likely to be holding (e.g., deep ledges, weed edges, or rocky points).
- **Presentation Techniques**: Advising on retrieval speeds and depth control based on metabolic states.

### 📱 Built for the Field (PWA)
Designed as a **Progressive Web App (PWA)**, FishSmart Pro is built to perform where the fish are. 
- **Deep Ocean Aesthetic**: A high-contrast, dark-mode interface optimized for outdoor visibility.
- **Field Reliability**: Service Worker integration ensures the platform remains stable and responsive even in areas with marginal connectivity.
- **Cross-Platform**: Install directly to your home screen on iOS or Android without the bloat of traditional app stores.

### 🐟 Species-Specific Deep Dives
Our database contains comprehensive behavioral models for the most sought-after freshwater game fish, including:
- **Largemouth & Smallmouth Bass**
- **Walleye**
- **Northern Pike**
- **Crappie**
- **Trout Species**
- **Catfish**

To empower every angler with the same level of scientific insight used by professional biologists and elite tournament pros. With FishSmart Pro, you're not just fishing; you're solving the puzzle of the water.
